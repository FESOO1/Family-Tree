All about Family Tree

Purpose of Family Tree
  • Data storage.
  • People can upload all their (real) photos and videos of themselves to store them forever.
  • Gather people to one place so that they will have a chance to deep dive into their relatives that they may never have met before in real life.
Functions
  • Not only can you find out who your relatives are but you can also build a tree according to this data, it will also allow you to save that data as a photo or video so that you will be able to share it with other people.
